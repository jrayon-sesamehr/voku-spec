# A1 Lesson 05: Relations (-o Words) and Simple Sentences

## Learning Objectives
- Recognize that words ending in -o are relations (prepositions)
- Use eno (in), kono (with), and poro (for) in sentences
- Build complete sentences with entities, actions, qualities, and relations

---

## New Vocabulary

| Voku | English | POS |
|------|---------|-----|
| eno | inside, within, in | preposition |
| kono | with, together with | preposition |
| poro | for, on behalf of | preposition |
| eso | outside, out of | preposition |
| ano | above, more than, over | preposition |
| sino | without | preposition |
| vel | he/she/it (known third person) | pronoun |
| velri | they (known to both) | pronoun |
| solri-kon | we (inclusive, all of us) | pronoun |
| norri | you all (plural) | pronoun |

---

## Grammar Point: The -o Class (Relations)

Words ending in **-o** are always **relations** (prepositions). They connect entities to show spatial, social, or logical relationships.

| Voku | English |
|------|---------|
| eno | in, inside |
| eso | outside |
| kono | with |
| sino | without |
| poro | for |
| ano | above |

### Sentence Structure with Relations

```
[ka] + [Subject] + [Verb] + [Object] + [Relation] + [Entity]
```

Example:
```
Ka sol take toka kono nor.
DECL 1SG do task with 2SG
'I do the task with you.'
```

---

## Examples

1. **Ka sola eno hima.**
   - DECL sun in sky
   - The sun is in the sky.

2. **Ka sol take toka kono nor.**
   - DECL 1SG do task with 2SG
   - I do the task with you.

3. **Ka sol meke mesa poro vel.**
   - DECL 1SG create message for 3SG
   - I create a message for him/her.

4. **Ka ama eso tama.**
   - DECL water outside house
   - The water is outside the house.

5. **Ka sol take toka sino pera.**
   - DECL 1SG do task without error
   - I do the task without errors.

## Exercises

### Exercise 1: Match the relation
| Voku | English |
|------|---------|
| 1. eno | a) without |
| 2. kono | b) for |
| 3. poro | c) in |
| 4. eso | d) with |
| 5. sino | e) outside |

**Answers:** 1-c, 2-d, 3-b, 4-e, 5-a

### Exercise 2: Fill in the blank
1. Ka sol take toka ___ nor. (I do the task with you.)
2. Ka sola ___ hima. (The sun is in the sky.)
3. Ka sol meke mesa ___ vel. (I create a message for him.)

**Answers:** 1. kono, 2. eno, 3. poro

### Exercise 3: Translate Voku to English
1. Ka luna eno hima.
2. Ka solri-kon take toka kono velri.
3. Ka sol meke mesa poro norri.
4. Ka ama eso tama.

**Answers:** 1. The moon is in the sky. 2. We do the task with them. 3. I create a message for you all. 4. The water is outside the house.

### Exercise 4: Translate English to Voku
1. I search for information in the system.
2. She creates a message for me.
3. We do the task without errors.

**Answers:** 1. Ka sol tore kela eno teru. 2. Ka vel meke mesa poro sol. 3. Ka solri-kon take toka sino pera.

### Exercise 5: Sentence building
Use each relation word in a sentence:
1. eno: ___
2. kono: ___
3. poro: ___

**Sample answers:**
1. Ka sol eno tama. (I am in the house.)
2. Ka sol voke kono nor. (I speak with you.)
3. Ka sol take toka poro vel. (I do the task for him.)

### Exercise 6: Complete the sentence
1. Ka fira ___ tama. (Fire is inside the house.)
2. Ka vel sene kela ___ pera. (He perceives information without error.)
3. Ka sola ___ hima en luna ___ hima. (The sun is above the sky and the moon is in the sky.)

**Answers:** 1. eno, 2. sino, 3. ano / eno
