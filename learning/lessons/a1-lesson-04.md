# A1 Lesson 04: Qualities (-i Words) and Descriptions

## Learning Objectives
- Recognize that words ending in -i are qualities (adjectives)
- Describe entities using quality words
- Understand that qualities follow the entity they describe

---

## New Vocabulary

| Voku | English | POS |
|------|---------|-----|
| novi | new, recent | adjective |
| veri | true, correct | adjective |
| fasi | false, incorrect | adjective |
| rapi | fast, quick | adjective |
| tomi | big, large | adjective |
| puli | small, little | adjective |
| hoti | hot | adjective |
| koli | cold | adjective |
| neri | near, close | adjective |
| mali | bad, negative | adjective |

---

## Grammar Point: The -i Class (Qualities)

Words ending in **-i** are always **qualities** (adjectives). They describe entities.

| Voku | English |
|------|---------|
| vali | good |
| mali | bad |
| novi | new |
| tomi | big |
| puli | small |

### Word Order for Descriptions

In Voku, qualities come **after** the entity they describe:

```
Entity + Quality

kela    novi      → new information
tama    tomi      → big house
ama     koli      → cold water
```

### Multiple Qualities

Use **en** (and) between qualities:

```
tama tomi en novi   → a big and new house
ama koli en puri    → cold and pure water
```

---

## Examples

1. **Ka sola hoti.**
   - DECL sun hot
   - The sun is hot.

2. **Ka ama koli.**
   - DECL water cold
   - The water is cold.

3. **Ka tama de-nor tomi en novi.**
   - DECL house POSS-2SG big and new
   - Your house is big and new.

4. **Ka kela de-vel fasi.**
   - DECL information POSS-3SG false
   - His information is false.

5. **Ka toka de-sol rapi.**
   - DECL task POSS-1SG fast
   - My task is fast (quick).

## Exercises

### Exercise 1: Identify the qualities
Which words are qualities (-i words)?
*novi, mesa, rapi, take, tomi, sol, fasi, hima, koli, loka*

**Answer:** novi, rapi, tomi, fasi, koli

### Exercise 2: Fill in the blank
1. Ka sola ___. (The sun is hot.)
2. Ka tama de-sol ___. (My house is big.)
3. Ka kela ___. (The information is new.)

**Answers:** 1. hoti, 2. tomi, 3. novi

### Exercise 3: Translate Voku to English
1. Ka ama koli.
2. Ka toka de-nor rapi.
3. Ka mesa de-vel vali.
4. Ka loka neri.

**Answers:** 1. The water is cold. 2. Your task is fast. 3. His message is good. 4. The place is near.

### Exercise 4: Translate English to Voku
1. The moon is big.
2. The information is true.
3. My house is new and good.

**Answers:** 1. Ka luna tomi. 2. Ka kela veri. 3. Ka tama de-sol novi en vali.

### Exercise 5: Describe these entities
Add a quality word to each entity:
1. sola ___
2. ama ___
3. tama ___
4. toka ___
5. kela ___

**Sample answers:** 1. sola hoti, 2. ama koli, 3. tama tomi, 4. toka rapi, 5. kela novi

### Exercise 6: Three-category sort
Sort into Entities (-a), Actions (-e), Qualities (-i):
*kela, meke, novi, sola, take, fasi, mesa, hure, tomi, loka*

| Entities (-a) | Actions (-e) | Qualities (-i) |
|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| ? | ? | ? |

**Answer:**
| Entities (-a) | Actions (-e) | Qualities (-i) |
|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| kela, sola, mesa, loka | meke, take, hure | novi, fasi, tomi |
