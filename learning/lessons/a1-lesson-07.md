# A1 Lesson 07: Commands with to

## Learning Objectives
- Use the imperative mode particle to to give commands
- Make requests and instructions
- Know the three core modes: ka (state), ve (ask), to (command)

---

## Grammar Point: Imperative Mode (to)

Use **to** at the start of a sentence to give a command or make a request. The subject is usually **nor** (you), but can be any agent.

```
To nor take toka.
IMP 2SG do task
'Do the task.' / 'Execute the task.'
```

### Three Core Modes Summary

| Mode | Particle | Purpose | Example |
|------|----------|---------|---------|
| Declarative | ka | State a fact | Ka sol take toka. |
| Interrogative | ve | Ask a question | Ve nor take toka? |
| Imperative | to | Give a command | To nor take toka. |

### Combining Commands

Use **en** (and) to chain commands:

```
To nor aze tama en vine.
IMP 2SG open house and come
'Open the house and come in.'
```

---

## Examples

1. **To nor take toka.**
   - IMP 2SG do task
   - Do the task.

2. **To nor vine.**
   - IMP 2SG come
   - Come here.

3. **To nor aze tama.**
   - IMP 2SG open house
   - Open the house.

4. **To nor sure mesa poro sol.**
   - IMP 2SG send message for 1SG
   - Send me a message.

5. **To norri lare voku.**
   - IMP 2PL learn language
   - (All of you) learn Voku.

## Exercises

### Exercise 1: Identify the mode
What mode is each sentence? (ka / ve / to)
1. Ka sol sene sola.
2. Ve nor kele kela?
3. To nor vine.
4. Ka ama koli.
5. To norri take toka.

**Answers:** 1. ka (declarative), 2. ve (interrogative), 3. to (imperative), 4. ka (declarative), 5. to (imperative)

### Exercise 2: Turn into commands
Change these declaratives into commands:
1. Ka nor take toka. → ___
2. Ka nor voke kela. → ___
3. Ka nor aze tama. → ___

**Answers:** 1. To nor take toka. 2. To nor voke kela. 3. To nor aze tama.

### Exercise 3: Translate Voku to English
1. To nor vine eno tama.
2. To nor oze tama.
3. To nor sure mesa poro vel.
4. To nor ule kela.

**Answers:** 1. Come into the house. 2. Close the house (door). 3. Send a message for him. 4. Use the information.

### Exercise 4: Translate English to Voku
1. Walk to the place.
2. Send me the information.
3. Help him.

**Answers:** 1. To nor vike ano loka. 2. To nor sure kela poro sol. 3. To nor hele vel.

### Exercise 5: Mode transformation
Write the same sentence in all three modes:
Base: nor / take / toka

1. Declarative: ___
2. Interrogative: ___
3. Imperative: ___

**Answers:**
1. Ka nor take toka. (You do the task.)
2. Ve nor take toka? (Do you do the task?)
3. To nor take toka. (Do the task.)

### Exercise 6: Give instructions
Write a 3-step instruction set using to:

**Sample answer:**
```
To nor aze tama.        (Open the house.)
To nor tore kela.       (Search for the information.)
To nor sure kela poro sol. (Send the information to me.)
```
