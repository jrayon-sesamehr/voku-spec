# A1 Lesson 08: Past Tense (te-) and Simple Stories

## Learning Objectives
- Use the past tense prefix te- on verbs
- Tell simple stories about what happened
- Contrast past (te-) with present (zero/nu-)

---

---

## Grammar Point: Past Tense (te-)

To talk about the past, add **te-** before the verb:

```
Present: sol take toka.        → I do the task.
Past:    sol te-take toka.     → I did the task.
```

### Formation

```
te- + verb

te-take    → did
te-sene    → perceived/saw
te-kele    → knew
te-meke    → created
te-voke    → communicated
te-ane     → ate
te-supe    → slept
```

### In Sentences

```
Ka sol te-sene sola.
DECL 1SG PST-perceive sun
'I saw the sun.'

Ka vel te-meke mesa.
DECL 3SG PST-create message
'He/she created a message.'
```

---

## Examples

1. **Ka sol te-sene luna eno nila.**
   - DECL 1SG PST-perceive moon in night
   - I saw the moon at night.

2. **Ka vel te-ane fura.**
   - DECL 3SG PST-eat fruit
   - He/she ate fruit.

3. **Ka solri-kon te-take toka.**
   - DECL 1PL.INCL PST-do task
   - We did the task.

4. **Ka sol te-supe eno nila.**
   - DECL 1SG PST-sleep in night
   - I slept at night.

5. **Ka vel te-fine toka.**
   - DECL 3SG PST-finish task
   - He/she finished the task.

---

## Exercises

### Exercise 1: Make it past tense
Add te- to each verb:
1. sol take toka → sol ___ toka
2. nor sene sola → nor ___ sola
3. vel meke mesa → vel ___ mesa
4. sol ane fura → sol ___ fura

**Answers:** 1. te-take, 2. te-sene, 3. te-meke, 4. te-ane

### Exercise 2: Present or past?
Identify the tense:
1. Ka sol take toka. → ___
2. Ka vel te-sene luna. → ___
3. Ka nor te-meke mesa. → ___
4. Ka sol sene sola. → ___

**Answers:** 1. present, 2. past, 3. past, 4. present

### Exercise 3: Translate Voku to English
1. Ka sol te-voke kono vel.
2. Ka velri te-take toka eno zara.
3. Ka sol te-supe eno nila.
4. Ka nor te-fine toka.

**Answers:** 1. I communicated with him. 2. They did the task during the day. 3. I slept at night. 4. You finished the task.

### Exercise 4: Translate English to Voku
1. She saw the sun.
2. We ate fruit.
3. I searched for information.

**Answers:** 1. Ka vel te-sene sola. 2. Ka solri-kon te-ane fura. 3. Ka sol te-tore kela.

### Exercise 5: Tell a simple story
Complete the story by filling in the blanks:

```
Ka sol te-___ eno nila.          (I slept at night.)
Ka sol te-___ sola eno zara.     (I saw the sun in the day.)
Ka sol te-___ fura.              (I ate fruit.)
Ka sol te-___ toka.              (I did the task.)
Ka sol te-___ toka.              (I finished the task.)
```

**Answers:** supe, sene, ane, take, fine

### Exercise 6: Write your own past story
Write 3 sentences about yesterday using te-:

**Sample answer:**
```
Ka sol te-sene sola eno hima.     (I saw the sun in the sky.)
Ka sol te-take toka kono vel.     (I did the task with him.)
Ka sol te-supe eno tama de-sol.   (I slept in my house.)
```
