# A1 Lesson 06: Questions with ve

## Learning Objectives
- Use the interrogative mode particle ve to form questions
- Ask yes/no questions
- Understand the difference between ka (statement) and ve (question)

---

## New Vocabulary

| Voku | English | POS |
|------|---------|-----|
| ve | interrogative mode (I ask) | mode particle |
| hire | ask, inquire | verb |
| hele | help, assist | verb |
| pore | give, transfer | verb |
| ore | receive, get | verb |
| suke | like, enjoy | verb |
| vare | want, desire | verb |
| pote | can, be able to | particle |
| mu | no, not (negation) | particle |
| su | I confirm (confirmative mode) | particle |

---

## Grammar Point: Interrogative Mode (ve)

To ask a question, replace **ka** with **ve** at the start of the sentence. The rest stays the same.

### Statement to Question

```
Ka nor kele kela.     → statement: You know the information.
Ve nor kele kela?     → question:  Do you know the information?
```

### Answering Questions

- **Su** + [confirmation] = Yes
- **Mu** = No

```
Ve nor kele kela?        → Do you know the information?
Su. Sol kele kela.       → Yes. I know the information.
Mu. Sol mu-kele kela.    → No. I don't know the information.
```

### Negation: mu

Place **mu** before the word it negates:

```
Ka sol mu-kele kela.     → I don't know the information.
Ka toka mu-vali.         → The task is not good.
```

---

## Examples

1. **Ve nor take toka?**
   - Q 2SG do task
   - Do you do the task?

2. **Ve vel kele kela?**
   - Q 3SG know information
   - Does he/she know the information?

3. **Ve nor suke voku?**
   - Q 2SG like language
   - Do you like Voku?

4. **Ve nor pote hele sol?**
   - Q 2SG can help 1SG
   - Can you help me?

5. **Su. Sol pote hele nor.**
   - CONF 1SG can help 2SG
   - Yes. I can help you.

## Exercises

### Exercise 1: Turn statements into questions
1. Ka nor kele kela. → ___
2. Ka vel take toka. → ___
3. Ka nor suke ama koli. → ___

**Answers:** 1. Ve nor kele kela? 2. Ve vel take toka? 3. Ve nor suke ama koli?

### Exercise 2: Answer the questions
1. Ve nor runa? → Su. ___.
2. Ve nor kele voku? → Mu. ___.
3. Ve nor suke sol? → Su. ___.

**Answers:** 1. Su. Sol runa. 2. Mu. Sol mu-kele voku. 3. Su. Sol suke nor.

### Exercise 3: Translate Voku to English
1. Ve nor pote take toka?
2. Ve vel sene sola?
3. Ka sol mu-kele kela.
4. Ve norri suke voku?

**Answers:** 1. Can you do the task? 2. Does he see the sun? 3. I don't know the information. 4. Do you all like Voku?

### Exercise 4: Translate English to Voku
1. Do you need help?
2. Can he create a message?
3. I don't like the task.

**Answers:** 1. Ve nor neke hele? 2. Ve vel pote meke mesa? 3. Ka sol mu-suke toka.

### Exercise 5: Dialogue completion
Fill in the blanks:

```
A: Alo. ___ nor kele kela? Finu.     (Do you know the information?)
B: ___. Sol mu-kele kela. Finu.       (No. I don't know the information.)
A: ___ nor pote tore kela? Finu.      (Can you search for the information?)
B: ___. Sol pote tore kela. Finu.     (Yes. I can search for the information.)
A: Ka vali. Klosu.                     (Good. Goodbye.)
```

**Answers:** Ve, Mu, Ve, Su

Write your own questions using ve:
