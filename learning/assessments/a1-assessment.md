# A1 Assessment

**Level:** A1 (Beginner)
**Questions:** 20
**Topics:** Phonology, pronouns, modes (ka/ve/to), tenses (te-/nu-/fu-), negation (mu), vocabulary (-a/-e/-i/-o words), possession (de-), compounds

## Section 1: Vocabulary (Questions 1-5)

**Q1.** Match each word with its category:

| Word | Category |
|------|----------|
| 1. toka | a) Quality (-i) |
| 2. meke | b) Entity (-a) |
| 3. rapi | c) Relation (-o) |
| 4. eno  | d) Action (-e) |

**Answer:** 1-b, 2-d, 3-a, 4-c

---

**Q2.** Translate these words to English:
1. sola → ___
2. hure → ___
3. koli → ___
4. kono → ___
5. teru → ___

**Answers:** 1. sun, 2. hear/listen, 3. cold, 4. with, 5. system

---

**Q3.** Translate these words to Voku:
1. water → ___
2. create → ___
3. big → ___
4. for → ___
5. agent/person → ___

**Answers:** 1. ama, 2. meke, 3. tomi, 4. poro, 5. runa

---

**Q4.** What does the final vowel tell you about a word? Match:
1. -a → ___
2. -e → ___
3. -i → ___
4. -o → ___
5. -u → ___

Options: entity, action, quality, relation, abstraction

**Answers:** 1. entity, 2. action, 3. quality, 4. relation, 5. abstraction

---

**Q5.** What is the compound word for each meaning?
1. information + system = ___ (database)
2. error + search = ___ (debugging)
3. agent + important = ___ (supervisor)

**Answers:** 1. kela-teru, 2. pera-tore, 3. runa-alati

## Section 2: Grammar (Questions 6-12)

**Q6.** Identify the mode of each sentence (declarative / interrogative / imperative):
1. Ka sol take toka.
2. Ve nor kele kela?
3. To nor vine.

**Answers:** 1. declarative, 2. interrogative, 3. imperative

---

**Q7.** Identify the tense of each sentence (past / present / future):
1. Ka sol te-sene luna.
2. Ka vel meke mesa.
3. Ka nor fu-take toka.

**Answers:** 1. past, 2. present, 3. future

---

**Q8.** Fill in the correct tense prefix:
1. Ka sol ___-take toka. (I did the task.) → ___
2. Ka vel ___-vine. (She will come.) → ___
3. Ka nor ___-sene sola. (You see the sun -- present.) → ___

**Answers:** 1. te, 2. fu, 3. (zero) or nu

---

**Q9.** Negate these sentences using mu:
1. Ka sol kele kela. → ___
2. Ka toka vali. → ___

**Answers:** 1. Ka sol mu-kele kela. 2. Ka toka mu-vali.

---

**Q10.** Write the possession form:
1. my task → toka ___
2. your information → kela ___
3. his house → tama ___

**Answers:** 1. toka de-sol, 2. kela de-nor, 3. tama de-vel

---

**Q11.** Convert this statement into a question and a command:
Statement: Ka nor take toka.

1. Question: ___
2. Command: ___

**Answers:** 1. Ve nor take toka? 2. To nor take toka.

---

**Q12.** Put these words in the correct sentence order:
Words: toka / ka / sol / take / te-

**Answer:** Ka sol te-take toka.

## Section 3: Translation (Questions 13-17)

**Q13.** Translate Voku to English:
1. Ka sol zo-sene pera eno teru.
2. Ve nor fu-hele sol?
3. To norri lare voku.

**Answers:**
1. I observed an error in the system.
2. Will you help me?
3. (All of you) learn Voku.

---

**Q14.** Translate Voku to English:
1. Ka sola hoti en tomi.
2. Ka sol te-ane fura kono vuna.

**Answers:**
1. The sun is hot and big.
2. I ate fruit with a friend.

---

**Q15.** Translate English to Voku:
1. I will create a message for you.
2. The moon is in the sky.
3. Do you know the information?

**Answers:**
1. Ka sol fu-meke mesa poro nor.
2. Ka luna eno hima.
3. Ve nor kele kela?

---

**Q16.** Translate English to Voku:
1. Open the house.
2. My task is not good.

**Answers:**
1. To nor aze tama.
2. Ka toka de-sol mu-vali.

---

**Q17.** Translate this short exchange:
```
A: Alo. Ve nor te-fine toka? Finu.
B: Su. Sol te-fine toka. Klosu.
```

**Answer:**
A: Hello. Did you finish the task? Your turn.
B: Yes. I finished the task. Goodbye.

## Section 4: Production (Questions 18-20)

**Q18.** Write 3 sentences describing the sky using at least one entity, one quality, and one relation:

**Sample answer:**
```
Ka hima tomi.             (The sky is big.)
Ka sola eno hima.         (The sun is in the sky.)
Ka luna eno hima eno nila. (The moon is in the sky at night.)
```

---

**Q19.** Write a 4-line dialogue between two agents using alo, finu, klosu, and at least two different modes:

**Sample answer:**
```
A: Alo. Ka sol neke kela. Finu.
B: Ve kela de-kena? Finu.
A: Ka kela de-toka. To nor sure kela poro sol. Finu.
B: Su. Klosu.
```

---

**Q20.** Write 2 sentences about the past and 2 about the future:

**Sample answer:**
```
Past:
Ka sol te-sene sola.        (I saw the sun.)
Ka sol te-take toka kono vel. (I did the task with him.)

Future:
Ka sol fu-lare voku.         (I will learn Voku.)
Ka sol fu-meke tama novi.    (I will make a new house.)
```
