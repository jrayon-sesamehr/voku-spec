# A2 Assessment

**Level:** A2 (Elementary)
**Questions:** 20
**Topics:** All 10 modes, aspects (du-/fi-), evidentiality (zo-), certainty (-en), passive (-pu), subordination (ta...ti), comparison (ani/eni/uni), negation (mu/nul/ink), connectors

**Prerequisites:** A1 material (modes ka/ve/to, tenses te-/nu-/fu-, basic vocabulary, compounds, possession de-)

---

## Section 1: Modes and Particles (Questions 1-5)

**Q1.** Match each mode particle with its function:

| Particle | Function |
|----------|----------|
| 1. si | a) I want/desire |
| 2. na | b) If...then (conditional) |
| 3. vo | c) I confirm |
| 4. su | d) It is possible that |
| 5. de | e) One must / it is necessary |

**Answer:** 1-b, 2-d, 3-a, 4-c, 5-e

---

**Q2.** Identify the mode of each sentence:
1. Si kela veri, sol fu-meke mesa.
2. Na pera eno teru.
3. Vo sol fi-take toka.
4. De toren ha-luke teru.
5. Ko kela pati-veri.

**Answers:** 1. conditional, 2. potential, 3. volitive, 4. deontic, 5. concessive

---

**Q3.** Choose the correct mode particle:
1. ___ nor fu-take toka? (I ask: Will you do the task?)
2. ___ kela veri, sol fu-voke. (If the information is true, I will communicate.)
3. ___ sol fi-take toka. (I want to complete the task.)
4. ___ kela veri. (I confirm: the information is true.)
5. ___ pera eno teru. (It is possible there is an error in the system.)

**Answers:** 1. ve, 2. si, 3. vo, 4. su, 5. na

---

**Q4.** Translate these sentences with non-basic modes:
1. De nor voke kela poro sol.
2. Vo solri-kon meke teru novi.
3. Ko toka pati-vali, sol fu-take.

**Answers:**
1. You must communicate the information to me.
2. We want to create a new system.
3. I acknowledge the task is partially good, (but) I will do it.

---

**Q5.** Write one sentence using each mode:
1. re (corrective): ___
2. ko (concessive): ___

**Sample answers:**
1. Re sol te-voke kela-fasi. Ka kela veri. (I correct: what I said was false. The information is true.)
2. Ko nor te-take toka, sol mu-sati. (I acknowledge you did the task, but I'm not satisfied.)

## Section 2: Aspect and Evidentiality (Questions 6-10)

**Q6.** Match the aspect prefix with its meaning:
1. -du- → ___
2. -fi- → ___

Options: a) completed with result, b) ongoing/in progress

**Answers:** 1-b, 2-a

---

**Q7.** Add the correct aspect prefix:
1. Ka sol te-___-take toka. (I was doing the task -- in progress.)
2. Ka vel te-___-meke mesa. (She finished creating the message -- completed.)
3. Ka sol ___-sene pera. (I am observing the error -- in progress.)

**Answers:** 1. du, 2. fi, 3. du

---

**Q8.** What does zo- mean? Choose the correct answer:
a) I heard it from someone
b) I directly observed it
c) I calculated it
d) I assumed it

**Answer:** b) I directly observed it

---

**Q9.** Translate these sentences with evidentiality:
1. Ka sol zo-sene pera eno teru.
2. Ka sol zo-kele kela veri.

**Answers:**
1. I directly observed an error in the system.
2. I know from direct observation that the information is true.

---

**Q10.** Add zo- to make an observation statement:
1. Ka sol ___-sene teru mute. (I directly observe the system changing.)
2. Ka sol ___-kele kela novi. (I directly know the new information.)

**Answers:** 1. zo, 2. zo

## Section 3: Certainty, Voice, and Negation (Questions 11-15)

**Q11.** What certainty level does -en express?
a) Speculative (<30%)
b) Uncertain (30-60%)
c) Probable (60-95%)
d) Certain (>95%)

**Answer:** c) Probable (60-95%)

---

**Q12.** Translate sentences with certainty:
1. Ka sol li-kele-en kela veri.
2. Ka vel take-en toka.

**Answers:**
1. I probably know (by deduction) the information is true.
2. He/she probably does the task.

---

**Q13.** Convert to passive voice using -pu:
1. Ka sol take toka. → Ka toka ___. (The task is done.)
2. Ka vel meke mesa. → Ka mesa ___. (The message is created.)

**Answers:** 1. Ka toka take-pu. 2. Ka mesa meke-pu.

---

**Q14.** Match each negation type:
1. mu → ___
2. nul → ___
3. ink → ___

Options: a) I don't know (epistemic uncertainty), b) simple negation (not), c) nothing exists (empty)

**Answers:** 1-b, 2-c, 3-a

---

**Q15.** Choose the right negation:
1. Sol ___ kele kela. (I simply don't know the information.)
2. ___ pera eno teru. (There are no errors in the system -- none exist.)
3. ___ sol kele kela. (I don't know whether I know the information.)

**Answers:** 1. mu, 2. nul, 3. ink

---

## Section 4: Subordination and Comparison (Questions 16-18)

**Q16.** Translate sentences with subordination (ta...ti):
1. Ka sol take toka ta-kes nor te-voke poro sol kela ti.
2. Sol fu-take toka ta-mot nor fi-kele kela ti.

**Answers:**
1. I do the task because you communicated the information to me.
2. I will do the task so that you obtain the information.

---

**Q17.** Write sentences using ta...ti:
1. I don't do the task because there is an error. → ___
2. I will help you if you need it. → ___

**Answers:**
1. Ka sol mu-take toka ta-kes pera eno teru ti.
2. Ka sol fu-hele nor ta-si nor neke ti.

---

**Q18.** Translate comparison sentences:
1. Ka teru-nova rapi ani teru-ante.
2. Ka kela de-sol eni kela de-nor.

**Answers:**
1. The new system is faster than the previous one.
2. My information is equal to your information.

## Section 5: Production (Questions 19-20)

**Q19.** Write a 4-sentence technical report about a system error. Use at least: one evidential (zo-), one aspect (du- or fi-), and one subordination (ta...ti).

**Sample answer:**
```
Ka sol zo-sene pera eno teru.
(I directly observed an error in the system.)

Ka pera du-mute teru.
(The error is changing the system.)

Ka sol li-kele-en pera kes ta kela-omi mu-fi-mute-pu ti.
(I probably deduce the error is because old data was not fully updated.)

Vo sol fi-mute pera.
(I want to resolve the error.)
```

---

**Q20.** Write a dialogue between two agents (4 turns total). Use at least: conditional (si), confirmation (su), one comparison (ani/eni/uni), and passive voice (-pu).

**Sample answer:**
```
A: Alo. Ka toka-nova te-meke-pu. Ve nor zo-luke-en toka? Finu.
   (Hello. A new task was created. Have you probably evaluated the task? Your turn.)

B: Su. Ka sol zo-luke toka. Ka toka-nova alati ani toka-ante. Finu.
   (Yes. I observed and evaluated the task. The new task is more important than the previous one. Your turn.)

A: Si toka alati, de solri-kon fi-take toka rapi. Finu.
   (If the task is important, we must complete it quickly. Your turn.)

B: Su. Sol fu-fi-take toka. Klosu.
   (Confirmed. I will complete the task. Goodbye.)
```
