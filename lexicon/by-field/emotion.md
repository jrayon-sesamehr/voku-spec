## Derivation Patterns

The emotion vocabulary follows systematic derivation:

- **Adjective (-i)** describes the state: `hapi` = happy
- **Noun (-a)** names the emotion: `hapa` = happiness
- **Verb (-e)** for expression: `rile` = smile
- **Abstraction (-u)** for the concept: `tolu` = emotion, `risu` = humor

When the direct -i to -a derivation would collide with an existing word, the compound pattern `[adj]-tole` (literally "feeling-[adj]") is used instead.
